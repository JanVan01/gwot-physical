Project description

This study project was done within Web of Things course in Institute for Geoinformatics University of Muenster.

The goal of the project is to investigate the possibility of using low cost sensors in context of Web of Things for water level measurements.
Our team implemented the concept of "physical smart thing", what means that the device has all functionality
on it (e.g. server, database, mqtt broker, frontend templates, notifiers, setup).

Smart device based on Raspberry Pi was installed at Werse river. Measurements are also published via OpenSenseMap.
